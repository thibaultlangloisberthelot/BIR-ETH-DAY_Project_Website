The "BIR(E)THDAY" Project

The Ethereum Smart-Contracts based birthday gifts and Saving Accounts!

The Concept

Discover and then use smart-contract technology for predetermined beneficiaries - and to freeze funds for a fixed period of time - in order to be able to send them funds each year on their birthdays.

Open Source

The code of the Smart-Contracts can be found and re-used here, however, smart-contracts are an evolving technology and the security of ethereum funds can only be at the discretion of their owner.

In Real Time

You can track the transaction flows - incoming and outgoing - of these contracts directly on this website. The fund of this Project is dedicated to two specific person only.

Secure & Restricted Access!

Only predefined beneficiaries prior to the publication of smart-contracts will be able to successfully interact with their code.

Public

But anyone can look at the smart-contracts code and transactions, even the hackers, who shouldn't be able to steal funds successfully ;)

The "BALT" Smart-Contract




Explanation about the BALT smart-contract:




The address: It represents a "bank account" specifically dedicated to the Ethereum Blockchain (can only contain the "ethers" crypto-asset). Its access - to carry out transactions - is only possible to the holders of its private key (a code in the form of several words) associated with it. In this instance, the address represents the "account" of BALT's Smart-Contract.

The balance: It represents the total, in ethers, held by the Smart-Contract of Balt. When the funds release date is reached, the contract beneficiary will be able to automatically withdraw the funds to his own address.

Transfers: These correspond to the interactions - ether inputs and outputs - that are made with the contract. Anyone can try to interact with the latter, however, some actions - such as withdrawing funds - will not succeed if the contract doesn't meet certain conditions: the funds are in safe hands.



Use the bellow displayed application to convert the amount of ethers you see in the BALT's contract Balance, to estimate in euro the amount it hold.

Why this Proof of Concept (PoC) ?

The main purpose of this personal challenge was to put into practice the theoretical and technical knowledge of its author, through the use of smart-contract technology: it was a matter of achieving in a few days the development of two stable and secure contracts, according to welldefined concrete goals.
